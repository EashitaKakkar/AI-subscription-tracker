##  Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. How does SpendLens calculate my savings?
Our engine uses a live price ledger to compare your current monthly spend against optimized annual tiers and team-specific pricing for tools like Cursor, Claude, and ChatGPT.

### 2. Is my subscription data shared with anyone?
No. While we generate a unique shareable URL for you to show your team, all personally identifiable information (PII) is stripped from the public view. We only store the audit results using Supabase RLS policies for maximum security.

### 3. What if my tool isn't on the list?
We currently support the most popular AI development and productivity tools (Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Anthropic, OpenAI, Gemini, and v0). We are constantly updating our ledger to include new enterprise AI tools.

### 4. How does the AI Executive Summary work?
Once you complete the audit, we send your raw data to a specialized instance of Gemini Pro. It analyzes the overlap in your toolset and provides 2-3 strategic "wins" formatted for a professional financial report.

### 5. Can I export these results?
Currently, you can share your results via a unique link or capture the report via the lead-generation email sent through our Resend integration. Direct PDF export is on our roadmap for future updates.
